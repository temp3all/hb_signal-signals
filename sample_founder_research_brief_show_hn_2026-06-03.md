## Actionable takeaways for a founder

1. Devtool/AI launches still dominate fresh Show HN inventory; differentiation needs a concrete before/after demo, not generic “AI-powered” wording.
2. Low-comment launches need a tighter discussion hook: ask for specific feedback on setup friction, pricing, security model, or target user rather than “thoughts?”.
3. If the product is open-source, the HN post should make the repo outcome obvious in the first sentence: who installs it, what pain disappears, and how fast proof appears.
4. For paid/pro products, include one no-login proof path: demo video, public example, live sandbox, or screenshot walkthrough.
5. Best quick monetization angle from this feed: offer launch-page/README clarity fixes to devtool founders within 24h, using a public mini-audit as proof.
